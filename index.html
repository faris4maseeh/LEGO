The history of LEGO


We all love LEGO, which is suitable for everyone, no matter their age. But most of us don't know the true story of LEGO. In this website, I will tell you the amazing history of the world's most popular toy.



It all started in 1932. The LEGO group was founded by Ole Kirk Kristiansen. The company has since passed from generation to generation and is still owned by the Kirk Kristiansen family.



In 1958, the modern brick design was developed; ABS subsequently replaced cellulose acetate as the manufacturing material five years later. A patent application for the modern Lego brick design was filed in Denmark on 28 January 1958 and in various other countries in the subsequent years.



The Lego Group's Duplo product line was introduced in 1969, designed for younger children with larger blocks.



In 1978, Lego produced the first minifigures, which have since become a staple in most sets.



In February 2015, marketing consulting company Brand Finance ranked Lego as the "world's most powerful brand," overtaking Ferrari.
