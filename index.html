The history of LEGO


We all love LEGO is suitable for everyone no matter their age. But most of us dont know the true story LEGO. In this website I will tell you the amazing history of the worlds most popular toy.

It all started in 1932. The LEGO group was founded by by Ole Kirk Kristiansen. The company has since passed from generation to generation and is to this day still owned by the Kirk Kristiansen family.
